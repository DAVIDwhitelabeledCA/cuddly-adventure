Client Support

Care Designed for Your Recovery

VPACPlus and Joe’s Specialty Pharmacy communicate closely with your counsellors, case managers, and program staff to help ensure fewer gaps in care, safer medication use, and smoother transitions during discharge and aftercare.

Access to Medical & Mental Health Providers

  • Regular access to doctors, nurse practitioners, and mental health specialists
  • Support with ongoing medical needs, prescriptions, and follow-up care
  • Urgent appointments available when you need them

Mental Health Support

  • Psychiatric evaluations
  • Medication management
  • ADHD assessments
  • Coordination with therapists and counsellors

Primary Care Services

  • Prescription refills
  • Lab testing and results
  • STI testing
  • Medical forms and documentation
  • Urine drug screening when required

Onsite Nursing Support

Our nursing team may be available directly at your home or facility to provide:

  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Diabetes and insulin management
  • Wound care
  • Injections such as:
    • Sublocade
    • Long-acting mental health medications

Our Focus: Addiction Recovery

Joe’s Specialty Pharmacy focuses only on addiction recovery and mental health care. That means:

  • Accurate, reliable medication delivery
  • Clearly organized packaging to help you stay on track
  • Pharmacists who understand recovery and treat you with respect
  • Confidential, compassionate care

Medications are delivered safely and managed in coordination with your care team so you can focus on healing — not logistics.

Corporate Partnership

Comprehensive Clinic + Pharmacy Offering

Our multidisciplinary clinic model is built specifically for addiction treatment and mental health programs, providing streamlined medical access, consistent clinical oversight, and dependable support for both staff and residents. We don’t just support treatment — we become part of your team.

Medical & Clinical Services

Access to Providers

  • Five days a week access to general and specialty medical providers, with emergency availability
  • Simple, efficient scheduling for intakes, renewals, and ongoing primary care

Multidisciplinary Care Team

  • Addiction medicine physicians
  • Nurse practitioners
  • Psychiatrists & psychologists
  • Registered nurses

Medical & Behavioral Health Services

  • Rapid intake and priority scheduling
  • Comprehensive mental health assessments & ADHD evaluations
  • Emergency detoxification management (as clinically appropriate)
  • General medical services, including prescription refills, lab testing and results review, STI testing, medical forms and letters, and point-of-care urine testing
  • Urgent care access with physician appointments available within 24 hours
Pharmacy & Medication Services

Specialized Pharmacy Support

  • Dedicated pharmacists focused exclusively on addiction treatment and mental health homes
  • Open seven days a week with reliable daily medication delivery
  • Seasonal vaccination services
  • OAT-certified pharmacists

Customized Medication Management & Compliance Packaging

  • Automated compliance packaging (PACMED) to reduce medication errors
  • Safe, structured medication management systems
  • Medication storage solutions aligned with licensing and regulatory standards
  • Provision of Medication Administration Records (MARs) for accurate documentation
  • Privacy-focused, compassionate patient care

Collaboration & Staff Support

  • Seamless coordination between prescribers, pharmacy teams, and facility leadership
  • Customized onsite staff training and support
  • Guidance on MAR documentation, medication administration, intake and discharge processes, and patient education
